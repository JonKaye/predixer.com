Overview

Information We Collect

How We Use Your Information

What We Don't Do

  • We do not send marketing messages

SMS Messages

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive one-time verification codes via SMS. Message frequency depends on your login activity. Message and data rates may apply. You can stop using the service at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your account information while your account is active. You may request account deletion by contacting us.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections and secure authentication.
